## What It Does

CreatorPilot is an all-in-one monetization command center built specifically for content creators — YouTubers, streamers, podcasters, and influencers who earn money across multiple channels. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, email threads, and half a dozen disconnected tools, creators manage their entire business from a single, polished dashboard.

The app centralizes every revenue stream: a sponsorship CRM with a visual Kanban pipeline tracks brand deals from initial contact to signed contract; an affiliate program manager handles partner relationships, tracking links, and commissions; a lead capture system with funnel visualization converts inbound interest into deals; and digital product listings with AI-powered pricing guidance help creators sell courses, templates, and merch at the right price. A brand kit builder lets creators store and manage their media assets, audience stats, and rate cards so they're always ready to pitch.

Built-in AI tools (powered by OpenAI GPT-4o-mini) handle the busywork: suggested pricing for sponsorship packages, brand-to-creator matching scores, smart follow-up email drafts, content idea generation, and brand discovery to find new sponsorship targets. An analytics dashboard tracks everything, and email notifications via Resend keep creators informed without leaving the app. The whole thing ships as an installable PWA with offline support, light/dark themes, and a polished glass-morphism UI.

**It's fully built, never launched, and ready for a buyer to go live immediately.**
